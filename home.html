About Ponderosa Home Cooking

Ponderosa is the culmination of a deep history of soul food, music, and socializing in the deep South. We are located in the heart of Marietta at 1730 Calhoun Street. This is a place to relax, enjoy live music and great food. We offer a diverse culture with music from all genres and popular hits throughout American history. We have the best fried chicken and mac and cheese in the South. Our desert menu is full of your old famous Apple Pie, Peach Cobbler, and Pecan Pie with many more to choose from. This is the perfect place to whine down kick your feet up enjoy some good Southern cooking and listen to good music.
